Design a high-fidelity desktop-first landing page for “AutoHelper”, a modern SaaS platform with an AI automotive assistant and vehicle history management system.

Main goal:
Create a clean, premium, trustworthy, startup-style landing page that instantly explains the value of the product to non-expert car owners. Within the first 5 seconds, the user should understand:
“If something is wrong with my car, AutoHelper helps me understand the situation, avoid bad repair decisions, and stay in control.”

Brand personality:
- functional
- useful
- expert
- friendly
- reliable
- calm in stressful situations
- modern SaaS
- minimalistic and technological
- premium but accessible

Target audience:
Car owners aged 20–35, mostly non-experts, especially in Moldova and CIS markets. They are afraid of being cheated by repair shops, unsure how to react to car issues, and need a trustworthy AI guide.

Core product:
AutoHelper helps users:
- chat with an AI automotive assistant
- understand vehicle problems
- review repair/service situations
- manage their cars
- track vehicle service history
- keep records of completed works
- subscribe for advanced AI assistance

Landing page objective:
Convince the user to sign up and start using the AI assistant.

Main UX path:
Landing page → Sign up / Login → Go to AutoHelper chatbot

Style direction:
- light theme only
- clean, premium SaaS aesthetic
- trustworthy and calm
- subtle startup energy
- modern and structured, not generic
- no racing or tuning visual language
- no playful chatbot look
- strong product credibility
- elegant wow-effect through composition, polish, and product UI mockups

Color palette:
Use a refined light palette:
- cold light backgrounds
- deep navy for trust and primary CTA
- cyan / bright blue as AI accent
- green only for safe / success / verified states
Avoid warm beige tones, loud orange, overuse of neon, or too much pure white.

Suggested visual formula:
Ice white base + deep navy trust + intelligent cyan + controlled semantic green

Design system feel:
- spacious layout
- soft shadows
- subtle borders
- crisp cards
- clean typography hierarchy
- slightly futuristic but still credible
- high readability
- modern product storytelling

Create the landing page with these sections in this order:

1. Header
- text-based logo placeholder “AutoHelper”
- navigation links
- language switcher (RU / EN)
- login button
- primary CTA button

2. Hero section
- strong headline about solving stressful car situations with AI
- supportive subheadline focused on clarity, confidence, and avoiding bad repair decisions
- primary CTA: Start with AutoHelper
- secondary CTA: See how it works
- product mockup / hero visual on the right:
  show a premium UI composition with:
  - AI chatbot window
  - diagnostic result card
  - vehicle summary card
  - service history card
  - trust/safety badges

3. Pain points section
Show the main fears of users:
- I don’t understand what is wrong with my car
- I’m afraid the service center will overcharge me
- I don’t know whether the repair is really necessary
- I don’t keep a clear record of my car history
Use clean icon cards or split content blocks.

4. Solution / how AutoHelper helps
Explain how the product solves those problems:
- AI explains what may be wrong
- helps understand repair situations
- keeps car history organized
- helps the user make more confident decisions
Use 3–4 premium feature cards.

5. How it works
Create a simple 4-step flow:
- Describe the problem
- Get AI guidance
- Track your vehicle history
- Make smarter service decisions
Use a clean visual step layout.

6. AI assistant showcase
This block is critical.
Present AutoHelper as a specialized automotive AI tool, not a generic chatbot.
Show:
- structured AI response cards
- probable issue
- urgency
- recommendations
- next steps
- linked car context
- safe / warning status indicators
Use a realistic product-style interface mockup.

7. Benefits / advantages section
Highlight platform strengths:
- expert guidance in stressful moments
- transparency and better repair decisions
- vehicle history in one place
- simple and convenient interface
- subscription-based AI help
Use a grid of benefit cards.

8. Use cases / scenarios
Show realistic scenarios such as:
- strange engine noise
- unclear repair quote
- checking service history
- understanding if the car is safe to drive
Create visual cards with short scenario summaries.

9. Pricing section
Create 3 pricing cards for AI subscription plans:
- Regular
- Pro
- Maximum
Make one plan visually featured.
Style should feel premium but simple, not overly salesy.
Include short bullets and CTA buttons.

10. Testimonials / reviews
Use clean testimonial cards with placeholder avatars, names, and short quotes.
The goal is trust and reassurance, not social-media style hype.

11. FAQ preview
Accordion-style block with 5–6 common questions.
The tone should feel clear, reassuring, and explanatory.

12. Final CTA section
Strong closing headline and CTA to sign up and start using AutoHelper.

13. Footer
Include:
- logo placeholder
- navigation links
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Service
- Cookie Policy
- support email
- social links placeholders
- copyright

14. Cookie banner
Elegant light-theme cookie consent banner, matching the design system.

Important content and UX constraints:
- no real logo yet, use text-based branding
- no team photos
- no stock mechanic photos
- rely on product UI, clean illustrations, icons, abstract AI-tech visuals
- design for desktop first, but keep layout responsive and modern
- multilingual-ready interface
- emphasize trust, expertise, calmness, and clarity
- the chatbot is the flagship product and should visually dominate the landing
- make it feel like a real premium SaaS startup, investor-ready and conversion-focused

Typography:
Use modern sans-serif typography with strong hierarchy.
Headlines should feel confident and premium.
Body text should be readable and calm.

Overall result:
A polished, high-converting, light-theme SaaS landing page for AutoHelper that feels trustworthy, expert, modern, and clearly centered around the AI automotive assistant.
